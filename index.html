Love can sometimes feel like an exhausting dance as you step towards connection, love and intimacy – only to then take a step back because you feel smothered and need to regain your space.

You’re often very generous with your energy, time and support because of your empathetic nature. But sometimes you can “over-give”, feeling like you’ve been taken advantage of and that your needs, wants and opinions don’t really matter.

As a fearful avoidant, your superpower is your ability to read other people. But this also means that you might overanalyze your partner’s behavior, micro-expressions and body language – always scanning for signs of betrayal and feeling uneasy.

You set impossibly high standards for yourself because you believe you need to be perfect before you can be fully loved. You’re also prone to feeling excessive guilt, which only reinforces your belief that something is wrong with you.
